How to Play

Here are the instructions to play Tic-Tac-Toe:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns marking a square with either an "X" or an "O".
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all squares are filled and no one wins, the game is a draw.
  • Click a square to place your mark.

Have fun playing!
